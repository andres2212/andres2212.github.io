                     Learn ASL Alphabet App 
                    
                    Welcome to the Learn ASL Alphabet app, your interactive companion for mastering the American Sign
                    Language (ASL) alphabet. Through a intuitive design and technology, our app
                    facilitates a dynamic learning experience, making ASL education accessible and engaging.

                     Features: 
                    Interactive Learning: Explore the ASL alphabet through our visual interface. Click on any letter to
                    unveil its corresponding ASL sign along with a video demonstration.

                    Real-Time Sign Recognition: Powered by YOLO v8 detection model, our app offers real-time
                    sign recognition through your webcam. Practice your signs and get instant feedback, enhancing your
                    learning curve.

                    Night Mode: Ensure a comfortable learning with our day and night modes.

                    Responsive Design: Enjoy a seamless learning experience across desktop, tablet, or smartphone, with
                    our app's responsive design adjusting to fit your screen.
                    
                     Limitations:
                    Our current version is trained using static images, which allows for the recognition of static signs. However,
                    this means that dynamic signs, like the letters J and Z, which require movement to convey meaning, are not 
                    supported at this time.
                    
                     Getting Started: 
                    Navigate through the alphabet using the scrollable menu at the top of the page.
                    Click on a letter to unveil its ASL sign and watch the video demonstration.
                    Activate your webcam and practice the signs in real-time, receiving immediate feedback through our
                    sign recognition system.
                    Toggle between day and night modes to suit your preference.
                    
                     Contact:
                    For any inquiries, suggestions, or feedback, feel free to reach out at contact@learnasl.info
